LITTLE LONDON
ENGLISH SCHOOL

Inner Mongolia Little London English School was founded in 2001.
It is a training institution for the Sino-British joint venture social force
officially approved by the Inner Mongolia Education Department.

SCHOOL
HISTORY

Seventeen years of wind and rain, seventeen years of extraordinary roads, under the correct leadership of the Education Department, under the scientific guidance of the Board of Directors, and with the joint efforts of all the little Londoners, the Little London English School finally won the training market in Inner Mongolia. There are seven branch schools in Hohhot, nine teaching centers, and two branch schools in Baotou. The exclusive teaching centers of each branch are located in the main blocks of the urban area. Our school has more than 200 full-time Chinese teachers, more than 100 full-time and part-time foreign teachers, more than 15,000 square meters of building use, and more than 10,000 students.

In January 2016, Wanda Teaching Center and Jinqiao Teaching Center were established; in June 2016, Jinmao Teaching Center was established; in November 2016, Jinyu Teaching Center and Mingze Teaching Center were established; in June 2017, Wanjin Teaching Center Established; In July 2017, the East Coast International Teaching Center was established; in September 2017, the Mengxi Teaching Center was established; in January 2018, the Qiqiao National Teaching Center was established, which is another new milestone in school development. The school adheres to the "people-oriented" concept of running a school. Over the past 17 years since its establishment, the school has trained more than 70,000 students.
