ON ARRIVAL

HOW YOUR FIRST TWO WEEKS LOOK
DISCLAIMER: The below is for students signed up to our travel option

Day 1: Arrive in Beijing, settle into your hotel followed by a welcome meeting and a welcome dinner.
Day 2: Introduction to Little London English School followed by a team building session then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 3: Discover The Great Wall of China then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 4: Discover Tiananmen Square and The Forbidden City followed by Silk Market Shopping then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 5: Discover Bird's Nest Beijing National Stadium (2008 Olympic Stadium) and Water Cube / The Beijing National Aquatics Center followed by Beijing Zoo then a Chinese Class and dinner.
Day 6: Depart Beijing for Xi'an, Arrive in Xi'an then have a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 7: Discover The Terracotta Army / Terracotta Warriors and Horses then a Chinese Class and dinner.
Day 8: Discover Shaanxi History Museum and Xi'an Ancient City Wall then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 9: Depart Xi'an for Hohhot, Arrive in Hohhot then have dinner.
Day 10: Meet and greet Little London English School staff followed by Hohhot sight seeing then dinner.
Day 11: Explore Hohhot or just relax on this day off then have dinner.
Day 12: Nursery Student Training Day (Age 3-6) then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 13: Primary Student Training Day (Age 6-12) then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 14: Secondary Student Training Day (Age 12-16) then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 15: Observation of Chinese Teacher's Class followed by Observation of Experienced Foreign Teacher's Class then a Chinese class and dinner.
Day 16: Class Demonstration Day then dinner.
